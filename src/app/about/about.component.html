About Famazon

Welcome to Famazon, your number one source for all things rubber duckies. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of rubber duckies, with a focus on three characteristics: squeakiness, floatability, and adorableness.

Founded in 2023 by Quacky McDuckface, Famazon has come a long way from its humble beginnings in a bathtub in New York City. When Quacky first started out, his passion for making bath time fun again drove him to quit his day job, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the world and are thrilled to be a part of the quirky wing of the rubber ducky industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
